About Alilee

We are a Kenyan-based startup dedicated to solving the growing problem of tyre waste through sustainable innovation. Our mission is to repurpose waste tyre material—specifically tyre dust from the retreading process—into durable, eco-friendly products for construction, landscaping, and other industries.

By combining recycled rubber with polyurethane (PU) binders, we’re creating a cleaner future while reducing environmental pollution and generating green jobs. Our solution is simple, local, and scalable—designed to serve both the environment and the economy.
